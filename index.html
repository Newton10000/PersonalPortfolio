Newton's Investment Company

I noticed that most financial websites only offer the PE Ratios & PS Ratios for a given company on a one year term. Given the circumstances of the past year, I figured that supplying multi-year information for these ratios would be very useful. Microsoft Excel allows a quick refresh for the stock price & market caps of most companies, allowing the multi-year ratios to update automatically.

Data is organized by S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, & the DOW Composite. This website was put together and deployed utilizing GoDaddy & Microsoft Excel.

I worked on this and launched it shortly after working as a financial analyst. I had been putting my money in the stock market and couldn't find any in depth financial information that was downloadable to excel. I made these excel tools by myself, and figured making a website to distribute the information would be useful to others as well.
